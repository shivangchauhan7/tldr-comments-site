How It Works on Amazon

TLDR Comments has a dedicated Amazon scraper that reads product reviews including star ratings. It extracts the reviews from the page and sends them to your AI provider, which generates a balanced summary highlighting what buyers love and what they complain about.

Open any Amazon product page, click the TLDR Comments icon, and get the review summary. Make smarter shopping decisions in seconds.

What You Get

  • A balanced summary of product pros and cons from real reviews
  • Sentiment analysis — see the positive vs. negative review ratio at a glance
  • Key themes and recurring complaints highlighted
  • Choose Brief, Bullet Points, or Detailed summary styles
  • Works on Amazon.com, Amazon.in, Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.de, and more

Why Use TLDR Comments for Amazon?

Popular Amazon products can have hundreds or thousands of reviews. Reading them all is impossible, and the star rating alone doesn't tell the full story. TLDR Comments gives you an AI-generated summary that captures the key themes — construction quality, sizing issues, value for money, common defects — so you can make a confident purchase decision without the research burden.
