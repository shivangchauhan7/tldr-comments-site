Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

TLDR Comments is a Chrome extension that summarizes website comment sections using AI. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

Data Collection

TLDR Comments does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server owned or operated by us.

What Data Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using Chrome’s built-in storage API and never leaves your browser:

  • API keys: Your AI provider API key (Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) is stored locally and sent directly to your chosen AI provider when you request a summary.
  • User preferences: Your chosen AI provider, model, and summary style settings.
  • Summary history: Previously generated summaries are cached locally for faster loading on revisit.

What Data is Sent to Third Parties

When you click “Summarize”, the extension sends the publicly visible comments from the current webpage directly to your chosen AI provider (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) for summarization. This data is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider’s API — it does not pass through any server owned by us.

The processing of this data is governed by your chosen AI provider’s privacy policy:

  • Google Gemini: https://ai.google.dev/terms
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect any analytics or usage data
  • We do not use any tracking or telemetry
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not store any data on remote servers
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not run any code in the background when you are not actively using the extension

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions, used solely for its core functionality:

  • activeTab: To read comments from the current page when you click Summarize
  • storage: To save your settings and summary history locally
  • scripting: To inject the comment scraper when you request a summary
  • Host permissions: To read comments from supported websites

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
