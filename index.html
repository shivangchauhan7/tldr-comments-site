How It Works

1

Open Any Page with Comments

Navigate to a Reddit thread, YouTube video, Amazon product, or any page with a comment section.

2

Click the TLDR Comments Icon

Click the extension icon in your toolbar and hit "Summarize." That's it.

3

Get Your AI Summary with Sentiment

Read a concise summary with positive/negative/neutral sentiment analysis, instantly.
